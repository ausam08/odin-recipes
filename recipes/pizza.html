What is Pizza?

Pizza is a beloved dish that combines a variety of flavors and textures in a single, satisfying experience. At its core, pizza features a round, flat base made from dough that is typically baked until it's golden and crisp. This crust serves as a canvas for an array of toppings.

The classic pizza starts with a layer of tomato sauce, seasoned with herbs and spices like oregano and basil. From there, the pizza can be customized with a wide range of toppings, such as pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, and more.

Steps

  1. Spread the dough into a circular disk
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Spread cheese on the sauce
  4. Put in the oven for 20 minutes
