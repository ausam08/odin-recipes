Burger

What is a Burger?

A burger is a classic American dish that features a hearty patty, typically made from ground beef, nestled between two soft, often toasted, buns. The patty is usually seasoned and grilled or fried to perfection, creating a savory, juicy center with a satisfying texture.

The burger's basic structure includes the bottom bun, a layer of condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise, followed by the cooked patty. On top of the patty, you might find a slice of melted cheese, such as cheddar or American, which adds a creamy richness. Toppings often include fresh ingredients like lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, and onions, which add crunch and freshness.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Patty
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Toast the buns
  2. Put sauce on one of the toasted buns
  3. Put patty on the bun
  4. Add lettuce on top
  5. Put the other half of the bun on top
